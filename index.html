Seed Phrase Backup Validator

Security Tips

1 - Never generate, test, or validate your seed phrase on a device connected to the internet.

2 - This page can be downloaded and used completely offline (right-click to save).

3 - Do not use your main operating system to test backups; prefer an amnesic system such as Tails OS.

4 - Never take photos, screenshots, or store your seed phrase in any digital format (files, cloud, email).

5 - Never print your seed phrase, as printers may store it in memory even after printing.

6 - Avoid typing your seed phrase on shared devices or devices with an unknown usage history.

7 - Prefer open-source software that is widely audited and obtained from official sources.

8 - Create more than one physical backup of your seed phrase and store them in separate, secure locations.

9 - Any website, application, or person asking for your seed phrase is attempting a scam.

10 - Add an easy-to-remember passphrase to your wallet with 16 or more characters and store it separately from your seed phrase.

11 - If your seed phrase is ever exposed, consider it compromised and move your funds immediately.

12 - Don't Trust, Verify.
